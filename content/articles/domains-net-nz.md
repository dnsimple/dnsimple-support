# .NET.NZ Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .NET.NZ domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.NET.NZ](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/net-nz-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .NZ domains (including .NET.NZ), see the [.NZ Domains](/articles/domains-nz/) article.

## Registering a .NET.NZ domain {#registering}

.NET.NZ is intended for network infrastructure providers and related organizations. Registration is open to organizations worldwide, though the domain is primarily used by New Zealand-based network providers.

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .NET.NZ domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
