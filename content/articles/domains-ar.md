# .AR Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for [.AR country-code domains](https://nic.ar/) when registered through DNSimple.

## Trustee service {#trustee}

A trustee service is required to register and manage .AR domains through our registrar. It is applied automatically to registrations.

If you want to move an existing .AR domain to DNSimple, the process is manual and involves updating owner data at the registry so the trustee can accept management. See [Transferring an existing .AR domain](#transferring).

## Registration and renewal {#registration}

.AR domains are registered for a monthly or yearly term. Registrations may go through a manual fulfillment step depending on registry requirements, so they are not always completed in real time.

Explicit renewal commands are not used for this TLD. To keep a domain active, use automatic renewal (or a one-time renewal mode) on the domain or as your account default, as supported in your DNSimple account.

Domains cannot be deleted with a standard delete command. To stop renewing a domain, use automatic deletion or expire mode according to your account settings and registry rules.

## Name server restrictions {#nameservers}

The registry does not allow two name servers to use the same IP address for a .AR domain.

## Contact updates {#contacts}

Contact changes for .AR are processed for internal registry purposes only and may not reflect a full public registrant update in the same way as open gTLDs.

## Transferring an existing .AR domain {#transferring}

Inbound transfers to our registrar are not available as a standard automated transfer. The .AR registry does not follow the typical inter-registrar transfer model.

To manage an existing .AR domain with us, the owner data must be updated at [NIC Argentina](https://nic.ar/) (using the registrant’s CUIL or CUIT credentials). The registry publishes [instructions for domain transfer](https://nic.ar/es/ayuda/instructivos/transferencia-de-dominio). Only the first steps through assigning the trustee identity are required before our trustee can accept and we can manage the domain. Contact [DNSimple support](https://dnsimple.com/feedback) if you need help.

Restore after expiration is not available for .AR.

## Internationalized domain names (IDN) {#idn}

Spanish and Portuguese characters are allowed in .AR domain labels, per registry IDN policy.
