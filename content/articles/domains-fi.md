* TOC
{:toc}

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .FI domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.FI](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/fi-domains) domains.

## Registering a .FI domain {#registering}

.FI domains are open for registration to individuals and organizations worldwide.

### For organizations

Organizations registering a .FI domain may need to provide:
- A business ID (Y-tunnus) if the organization is registered in Finland
- Company registration details

### For individuals

Individuals can register .FI domains with standard contact information.

## Have more questions?

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .FI domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
