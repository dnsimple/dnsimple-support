This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .BZH domain names.

## Registering a .BZH domain {#registering}

To register and renew a .BZH domain, the registrant must meet at least one eligibility condition:

- Have a mailing address in Brittany (Cotes d'Armor, Ille-et-Vilaine, Finistere, Morbihan, or Loire-Atlantique)
- Publish a website with a significant and original section about Brittany or Breton culture within six months of registration
- Publish a website with a significant and original section in Breton or Gallo within six months of registration
- Be a member of the `.bzh` association (`www.pik.bzh`)
