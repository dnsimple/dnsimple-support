# .GEN.NZ Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .GEN.NZ domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.GEN.NZ](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/gen-nz-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .NZ domains (including .GEN.NZ), see the [.NZ Domains](/articles/domains-nz/) article.

## Registering a .GEN.NZ domain {#registering}

.GEN.NZ is a general-purpose third-level domain under the .NZ namespace. Registration is open to individuals and organizations worldwide.

## Have more questions?

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .GEN.NZ domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
