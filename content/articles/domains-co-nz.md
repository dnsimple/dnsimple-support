# .CO.NZ Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .CO.NZ domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.CO.NZ](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/co-nz-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .NZ domains (including .CO.NZ), see the [.NZ Domains](/articles/domains-nz/) article.

## Registering a .CO.NZ domain {#registering}

.CO.NZ is intended for commercial organizations. Registration is open to organizations worldwide, though the domain is primarily used by New Zealand-based commercial entities.

## Have more questions?

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .CO.NZ domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
