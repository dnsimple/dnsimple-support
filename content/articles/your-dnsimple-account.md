Your DNSimple **account** holds your subscription, domains, billing, and team settings. You sign in as a **user** with your user email. On eligible plans, other people join as **members**. This page is the starting point for Account documentation.

For a guided first login, see [First Steps With Your DNSimple Account](/articles/guide-dashboard/). For how users, accounts, and members fit together, see [Users, Accounts, and Members at DNSimple](/articles/users-accounts-and-members-at-dnsimple/).
