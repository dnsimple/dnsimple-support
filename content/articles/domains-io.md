This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .IO domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.IO](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/io-domains) domains.

## Registering a .IO domain {#registering}

.IO domains are open for registration to individuals and organizations worldwide. The .IO TLD is very popular in the internet and tech industry, often used for technology companies, startups, and developer tools.
