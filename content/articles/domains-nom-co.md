# .NOM.CO Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .NOM.CO domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.NOM.CO](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/nom-co-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .CO domains (including .NOM.CO), see the [.CO Domains](/articles/domains-co/) article.

## Registering a .NOM.CO domain {#registering}

.NOM.CO is the personal/nom de plume third-level domain under the .CO namespace. Registration is open to individuals worldwide who want to register a personal domain name.

## Have more questions?

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .NOM.CO domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
