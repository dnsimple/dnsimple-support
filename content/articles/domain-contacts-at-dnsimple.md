### Table of Contents {#toc}

* TOC

A **domain contact** holds the registrant details a registry requires for a domain: a name, postal address, phone number, and email address. Contacts belong to your **account**, not to a **user**, so adding someone as a contact gives them no access to your DNSimple account. One contact can be assigned to as many domains as you like. This page is the starting point for contact documentation.
