# .ORG.NZ Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .ORG.NZ domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.ORG.NZ](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/org-nz-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .NZ domains (including .ORG.NZ), see the [.NZ Domains](/articles/domains-nz/) article.

## Registering a .ORG.NZ domain {#registering}

.ORG.NZ is intended for non-profit organizations, charities, and community groups. Registration is open to organizations worldwide, though the domain is primarily used by New Zealand-based non-profit organizations.

## Have more questions?

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .ORG.NZ domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
