This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .FR domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.FR](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/fr-domains) domains.

## Registering a .FR domain {#registering}

.FR domains are open for registration to individuals and organizations worldwide. However, there may be specific requirements depending on the registrant type.

### For organizations

Organizations registering a .FR domain may need to provide:
- A SIRET number (French business registration number) if the organization is registered in France
- Company registration details

### For individuals

Individuals can register .FR domains with standard contact information.
