DNSimple uses two different email addresses. Your **user email** is what you use to sign in. Each account has a **notification email** where DNSimple sends messages for that account, such as renewals and payment notices. For how users, accounts, and members relate, see [Users, Accounts, and Members at DNSimple](/articles/users-accounts-and-members-at-dnsimple/#user-email-and-notification-email).

## Changing the user email {#changing-the-user-email}

Your user email is tied to your personal DNSimple login. Changing it does not change the notification email on any account.

<div class="section-steps" markdown="1">
##### To change your user email

1. Go to your [user page](https://dnsimple.com/user) by clicking the account switcher at the top-right corner of the screen, then select <label>User settings</label>.

    ![Screenshot of link to user settings](/files/user-settings-menu.png)

1. In your user settings, select <label>Edit email</label>.

    ![Screenshot of changing email](/files/change-email.png)

1. Enter your current password and the new email address.
1. Confirm your new email, and click <label>Save</label>.

    ![Enter email address](/files/account-change-email-2.png)

1. A verification email will be sent to the new address. Your email address will not change until you complete the verification process.

    ![Screenshot of user settings after requesting an email change](/files/user-pending-email-change.png)

1. Open the verification email and click the link to confirm the new address. Once verified, your email address will be updated.

</div>

> [!NOTE]
> Your current email address remains active and fully functional until the new one is verified. If you have not received the verification email, you can request a new one from your user settings page. Verification links expire after 7 days. If yours has expired, requesting a new one will send a fresh link.

## Setting or changing the notification email {#setting-or-changing-the-notification-email}

The notification email is where DNSimple sends account messages for a specific account. It does not change your user email or where you sign in.

<div class="section-steps" markdown="1">
##### To set or change the notification email

1. Click the <label>account switcher</label> in the upper right-hand corner of the page.
1. Click the gear icon next to the account you want to update, then open <label>account settings</label>.
1. On the <label>Notification</label> card, click <label>Edit</label>.

    ![Screenshot of Notification card](/files/notification_card.png)

1. Enter the email address where you want account notifications to go.

    ![Screenshot of edit notifications](/files/edit_notifications.png)

1. Click <label>Save</label> to save your changes.

</div>

> [!NOTE]
> If you do not set a notification email, all account administrators receive notifications by default. On [eligible plans](https://dnsimple.com/pricing), you can also set a separate billing email. See [Billing Settings](/articles/billing-settings/#changing-the-billing-email).
