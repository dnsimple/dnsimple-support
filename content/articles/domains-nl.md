This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .NL domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.NL](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/nl-domains) domains.

## Registering a .NL domain {#registering}

.NL domains are open for registration to individuals and organizations worldwide.

### For organizations

Organizations registering a .NL domain may need to provide:
- A KVK number (Dutch Chamber of Commerce registration number) if the organization is registered in the Netherlands
- Company registration details

### For individuals

Individuals can register .NL domains with standard contact information.

If you have any questions about registering, transferring, or renewing your .NL domain, [contact us](https://dnsimple.com/feedback), and we'll be happy to help.
