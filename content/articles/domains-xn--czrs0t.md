# .商店 Domain Names (`xn--czrs0t`)

* TOC
{:toc}

---

**On the wire**, this TLD is represented as **`xn--czrs0t`**. The Unicode label is **商店** (Chinese for “shop”). Requirements below follow [CentralNic Reseller TLD documentation](https://kb.centralnicreseller.com/domains/tlds/xn--czrs0t).

## Registry restrictions {#restrictions}

### Sanctions (OFAC) validation

The registry validates contact addresses against U.S. OFAC sanctions rules. Certain **country and state/province** combinations are not allowed.

### Premium and reserved names (allocation token)

Reserved or premium names may require an **allocation token** issued by the registry. Provide it when prompted during registration if you received one.

## Transfers and auth codes {#transfers}

Auth codes must be **8–32** characters and include at least one letter, one number, and one allowed special character (see registrar documentation). New registrations are **transfer-locked for 60 days** at the registry.

## Internationalized domain names (IDN) {#idn}

Use the correct **IDN language tag** for your script when registering (see CentralNic KB and [IANA IDN tables](https://www.iana.org/domains/idn-tables)).
