# .NET.CO Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .NET.CO domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.NET.CO](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/net-co-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .CO domains (including .NET.CO), see the [.CO Domains](/articles/domains-co/) article.

## Registering a .NET.CO domain {#registering}

.NET.CO is the network infrastructure third-level domain under the .CO namespace. Registration is open to individuals and organizations worldwide, though the domain is primarily used by network providers and related organizations.
