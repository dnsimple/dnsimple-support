# .COM.CO Domain Names

* TOC
{:toc}

---

This article explains the requirements and special procedures for .COM.CO domain names. DNSimple supports registration, renewal, and transfer of [.COM.CO](https://dnsimple.com/tlds/com-co-domains) third-level domains.

For information about renewals, transfers, and ownership changes that apply to all .CO domains (including .COM.CO), see the [.CO Domains](/articles/domains-co/) article.

## Registering a .COM.CO domain {#registering}

.COM.CO is the commercial third-level domain under the .CO namespace. Registration is open to individuals and organizations worldwide, though the domain is primarily used by commercial entities.
